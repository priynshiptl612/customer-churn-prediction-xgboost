# 📉 Customer Churn Prediction & Retention Analysis

## 🚀 Project Overview

This project focuses on predicting customer churn using machine learning techniques and identifying customers who are at risk of leaving. The objective is to help businesses improve customer retention strategies through data-driven insights.

The solution leverages predictive analytics to classify customers as likely to churn or remain active, enabling proactive customer engagement and retention planning.

---

## 🎯 Business Problem

Customer churn directly impacts business growth and revenue. Identifying customers who are likely to leave allows organizations to:

* Improve customer retention
* Reduce revenue loss
* Increase customer lifetime value
* Optimize marketing efforts
* Enhance customer satisfaction
